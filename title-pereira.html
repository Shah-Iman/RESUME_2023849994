Middleweight Championship

Alex Pereira captured UFC gold at middleweight with devastating power. Using elite striking and precise timing, he secured a knockout victory to become UFC Middleweight Champion, completing a rapid rise in the division.

Move to Light Heavyweight

After moving up in weight, Pereira wasted no time making history again. A dominant performance and brutal finish earned him the UFC Light Heavyweight Championship, making him a two-division champion.
