Ilia Topuria – Title Fight Journey

Featherweight Championship

Ilia Topuria made history at featherweight with a composed and dominant title performance. Showing sharp boxing and elite control, he pressured his opponent from the opening bell. A clean, powerful sequence ended the fight, crowning Topuria as the UFC Featherweight Champion and proving his ability to finish at the highest level.

Move to Lightweight & Title Victory

After conquering featherweight, Topuria moved up to lightweight in search of a greater challenge. Facing elite competition, he displayed the same calm confidence and precision. One explosive moment changed everything, as Topuria delivered a first-round knockout to capture the UFC Lightweight Championship and remain undefeated.
