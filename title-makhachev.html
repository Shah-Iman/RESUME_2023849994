Lightweight Championship

Islam Makhachev captured the UFC Lightweight Championship with dominant grappling control and elite fight IQ. His pressure, composure, and finishing ability established him as the undisputed leader of the division.

Move to Welterweight & Championship Victory

After ruling lightweight, Makhachev moved up to welterweight seeking double-champion status. Facing larger opponents, he displayed the same technical dominance, securing victory to become UFC Welterweight Champion and cement his legacy as a two-division king.
